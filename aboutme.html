About Me

Currently I am attending Collin College and expect to graduate with my Associate's of Science after the Fall semester of 2022. I do plan to transfer to another university to pursue a degree in engineering or product design. In between semesters I plan to obtain my A+ certificate, and work as some sort of computer or electronics technician. I also have several ideas for products that I would like to bring to fruition soon.

My interests are eclectic and vary from time to time. I often find myself learning something new or having an idea that requires additional knowledge and fully investing into it for weeks to months at a time. Though it is not healthy, I often find myself forgetting to eat, sleep, and drink when I am fully invested in a project or learning endeavor. I get so absorbed in asking myself questions and challenging myself to find answers and solve problems, that I have spent many sleepless nights reading manufacturing spec sheets, sifting through tutorials, reviews, and watching many long-form videos about the in-depth mechanics of the topic I'm researching.

Currently, the interests that I have taken on as hobbies include prop works, electronics and electronics repair, video games, computers and computer building, 3D printing and 3D modeling, photography, and audio gear.

Many of these hobbies have even turned into business opportunities, and I was managing an online business doing 3D modeling, 3D printing, electronics, and prop work at the age of 14. Since then I have slowly gotten more and more busy in life and now only occasionally accept commissions when I have the free time and a market to capitalize on. I am 24 as of the time of writing and have kept up with my main hobbies all this time and now have quite a bit of experience in many of these areas.

I'm not one to hoard knowledge, or turn down a great job opportunity. If you would like to ask any questions, or like my work and would like to hire or commission me, please fill out the contact form.
