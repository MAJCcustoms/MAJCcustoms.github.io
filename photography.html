Photography

I am only beginning to get into photography, and I don't dedicate a bunch of time to it, but I find that it's a nice change of pace from everything else I do and helps me relax. I like portraits and contrast and most of my pictures are taken because I like the color contrast or bokeh of a scene.

Recently I've been taking some of what I call "moving pictures". They are essentially slow motion looped videos or gifs of a picture I would have taken, but wanted to capture the extra essence of motion in some other way than a slow shutter speed. I really like these, and with ambient sounds, I could create a sense of immersion for the viewer.

I am just a beginner, so I don't think any of my work in this area is great, but I do like learning more about the field so if you have any tips please contact me and let me know how to improve.

Enjoy some pictures.
